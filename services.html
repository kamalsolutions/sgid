Our Services

Business Development

SGID supports midsize business owners and managers with services that improve the performance of their enterprises, their access to markets and their competitiveness.

Our focus is both strategic and operational optimization as well as business process enhancements.

We leverage our operational expertise, our ability to put in the right systems and processes to help scale businesses.

Deal Origination

Amid competition for deals, we leverage unique deal origination strategies to give you an upper hand.

If you are either an active corporate seeking the next acquisition target or a private equity house contemplating a further investment in West Africa, a suitable target may not always be apparent, even when you have the funds available, the acquisition strategy decided and the geographical region agreed.

The market is imperfect and knowledge of the active buyers and sellers is vital.

SGID works with teams within West Africa who specialize in bringing that next target to you. We have set up mechanisms to recreate regular deal flows within the sub region.
